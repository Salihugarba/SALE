About Ammar Bin Yaseer Islamiyya

A community-rooted centre for Qur'an and Islamic learning in Yalwa Danziyal, Rimin Gado LGA, Kano State.

Our Story

Named after a companion, built for our community

Ammar Bin Yaseer Islamiyya takes its name from Ammar bin Yaseer (may Allah be pleased with him), one of the earliest and most steadfast companions of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. In that same spirit of patience and devotion to knowledge, our Islamiyya was founded to serve the families of Rimin Gado LGA and the wider Kano community.

We provide a structured path of learning — from the first letters of the Qur'an to full memorization, alongside Tajweed, Aqeedah, Fiqh and Arabic — delivered by dedicated teachers in a calm, disciplined environment for both children and adults.

Our Mission

To raise students who memorise and understand the Qur'an, embody good character, and carry sound Islamic knowledge into their homes and community.

Our Vision

To be a trusted centre of Qur'anic and Islamic education for every family in Rimin Gado LGA and beyond.

What Guides Us

Our Values

Sincerity (Ikhlas)

Every lesson is taught and learned for the sake of Allah alone.

Patience & Discipline

Memorization is built step by step, with consistent revision and care.

Good Character (Akhlaq)

Manners and character are taught alongside knowledge, not after it.

Community

We work closely with parents and elders to support every student's growth.

How Learning Progresses

A Clear Path of Study

Stage One

Noorani Qaida and correct letter pronunciation — the foundation for every new student.

Stage Two

Qur'an reading with Tajweed rules, moving juz' by juz' with regular assessment.

Stage Three

Full Hifz track with daily memorization, muraja'ah, and Islamic studies classes.

Stage Four

Advanced students continue into deeper Fiqh, Hadith and Arabic language study.
