Who We Are

A home for the Qur'an in Rimin Gado

Named after the noble companion Ammar bin Yaseer (may Allah be pleased with him), our Islamiyya was established to give children and adults in our community a solid grounding in the Qur'an, Tajweed, Islamic manners and the Arabic language — taught with patience, discipline and sincerity.

Qur'an First

Memorization and recitation form the core of every level.

Sound 'Aqeedah

Belief and character built on the Qur'an and authentic Sunnah.

For All Ages

Classes for children, youth and adult beginners.

What We Teach

Our Core Programmes

A structured path from the first letters of the Qur'an to full Hifz and Islamic scholarship.

01

Qur'an Memorization (Hifz)

Structured daily memorization and revision (muraja'ah) under close supervision.

02

Tajweed & Qira'ah

Correct articulation and rules of recitation, from Noorani Qaida to advanced Tajweed.

03

Islamic Studies

Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah and Hadith taught at a level suited to each student.

04

Arabic Language

Reading, writing and grammar to help students understand the Qur'an and Sunnah directly.
