Our Programmes

A full path of Qur'anic and Islamic education for children, youth and adults.

01

Qur'an Memorization (Hifz)

Daily memorization targets with structured muraja'ah (revision) so students retain what they memorise for life.

02

Tajweed & Qira'ah

From Noorani Qaida for beginners to advanced Tajweed rules, with one-on-one correction of recitation.

03

Islamic Studies

Aqeedah (creed), Fiqh (jurisprudence) and Seerah (the Prophet's biography) taught at an age-appropriate level.

04

Hadith Studies

Introduction to the sayings of the Prophet ﷺ, with explanation and everyday application.

05

Arabic Language

Reading, writing and grammar lessons so students can engage directly with the Qur'an and classical texts.

06

Adult & Evening Classes

Flexible sessions for working adults and parents who want to begin or continue their own Islamic education.
